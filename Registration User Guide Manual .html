Registration & Login Manual

1️⃣ How To Register

  • Enter your active email address.
  • Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters).
  • Confirm your password correctly.
  • Enter the verification code displayed on the screen.
  • Click Register Now.
📩 After registration, a verification email will be sent to your email address. You MUST open your email and click the verification link before you can login.
❗ If you do not see the verification email:
  • Check your Spam / Junk folder.
  • If found in Spam, click "Report Not Spam".
  • Then open the email and click the verification link.
Login will not work until your email is verified.

2️⃣ How To Login

  • Enter your verified email address.
  • Enter your correct password.
  • Click Login.
✅ Make sure your email has been verified before trying to login.
❌ If your email is not verified, you will see: “Please verify your email before logging in” ❌ If password is incorrect, login will fail.
