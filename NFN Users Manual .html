NFN Watch & Earn

Official User Guide & Instructions

1️⃣ Account Registration & Email Verification

  • Enter your email address.
  • Create a password (minimum 8 characters).
  • Confirm your password.
  • Enter the verification code shown.
  • Click Register Now.
📩 After registration, a verification email will be sent to your email.

You MUST open your email and click the verification link before login.
❗ If you do not see the email:
  • Check your Spam / Junk folder.
  • If you find it in Spam, click "Report Not Spam".
  • Then open the email and click the verification link.
Login will be blocked until email is verified.

2️⃣ Login

  • Enter your verified email.
  • Enter your password.
  • Click Login.
❌ If your email is not verified, you will see: “Please verify your email before logging in”

3️⃣ How To Earn

  • Go to Task section.
  • Watch videos completely.
  • Complete daily tasks.
  • Income is added automatically to your wallet.

4️⃣ Level Upgrade

  • Open Level tab.
  • Swipe to view levels.
  • Click Join Now to upgrade.
  • Higher levels = Higher daily income.

5️⃣ Recharge (Deposit)

  • Go to My → Recharge.
  • Enter the amount you want to deposit.
  • Select your preferred recharge channel (e.g., SwiftPay).
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the payment.
  • After payment, submit your details or session ID to confirm the transaction.
  • Your wallet will be updated once the payment is confirmed.
💡 Ensure you complete the payment within the time specified, and fill in the correct note or session ID when required.
❗ Do not transfer funds multiple times or use saved account details, as this may lead to loss of funds.

6️⃣ Personal Information & Bank Details

  • Go to My → Personal Information.
  • Update your profile picture and email address if necessary.
  • Click on Bank Card to add your bank details:
    • Select your bank from the list.
    • Enter your account name exactly as registered with the bank.
    • Enter your 10-digit account number (cannot be changed once saved).
    • Click Save Bank Card. Your bank info will be locked for security.
  • Set your 6-digit Withdrawal PIN under the Withdraw PIN section. You can change it later by verifying your old PIN.
  • Use the Invite Friends section to copy your referral link and share it for team rewards.
💡 All information must be accurate to ensure withdrawals and rewards are successful.
❗ Do not share your withdrawal PIN with anyone. Bank details cannot be changed after saving.

7️⃣ Withdrawal Process

  • Select a withdrawal amount.
  • Enter your 6-digit withdrawal PIN.
  • Click Submit.
💰 A 10% handling fee is automatically deducted from withdrawal amount.
Before withdrawing:
  • You MUST set your 6-digit withdrawal PIN in Personal Information.
  • You MUST bind your bank card in Personal Information.
  • If PIN is wrong, withdrawal will fail.
  • If bank card is not bound, withdrawal will be blocked.

8️⃣ Security Rules

  • No multiple accounts allowed.
  • No fake bank details.
  • Email verification is compulsory.
  • Do not share your withdrawal PIN.
  • Violation may lead to account suspension.

9️⃣ Referral & Team Rewards

  • Go to the Referral or Team section.
  • Copy your referral link or scan your QR code to invite friends.
  • Share the referral link via social media or messaging apps.
  • When your referrals register and complete tasks, you will earn rewards automatically.
📌 Each user has a unique referral code. Your referral link will look like: https://nfn-groups.github.io/referral-link-ref-NFN102938/?ref=YOURCODE
💎 Team Rewards:
  • Invite 2 A-level members → Reward 2,000
  • Cumulative 10 invitations in a week → Reward 5,000
  • Cumulative 20 invitations in a week → Reward 20,000
  • Cumulative 50 invitations in a week → Reward 50,000
